# Crypto-Is-Not-Money-It-s-a-System-Shift
Another important element of crypto is permissionless access. Anyone with an internet connection can participate, regardless of location, background, or financial status. This opens the door to a more inclusive financial system, where barriers to entry are significantly lower than in traditional finance. However, it also means that users must navigate risks on their own, without institutional protection.

Volatility is often seen as the defining feature of crypto, but it is only a surface-level characteristic. Prices fluctuate due to speculation, adoption cycles, macroeconomic conditions, and market sentiment. Beneath that volatility lies a deeper transformation—one that is less about price and more about control and infrastructure.
